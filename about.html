Our Story

How Funky Teens came to be and where we're headed

From a small dream to a big reality

It all started in 2020 when our founder, Raj Kumawat, noticed a gap in the market for trendy, affordable fashion for teens and young adults. With just ₹5000 and a passion for style, he started Funky Teens from his hostel room.

What began as a small Instagram page selling t-shirts has now grown into a full-fledged online store with over 500+ products across multiple categories. We've served more than 50,000 customers across 150+ cities in India.

Today, we're a team of 20+ passionate individuals working together to bring you the best in fashion and beauty.
